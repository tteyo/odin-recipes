Guide to Excellent Spaghetti

Description

Sometimes when I came home and had to feed my siblings with something quick, spaghetti was my bud because of its flexibility and ease

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add some water into a pot and bring to a boil with salt
  2. Portion out pasta and add into pot, boil until al dente
  3. Strain pasta out, separately bring sauce to a boil in another pot
  4. Combine pasta and sauce, mix in pot together
  5. Off heat and serve, for added points cook a protein of your choice beforehand and mix it in too
