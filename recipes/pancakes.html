Guide to Excellent Pancakes

Description

For early morning in which I wanted to allow myself some comfort while away from home. Can be done for yourself or as a treat for your special someone to enjoy wiht you

Ingredients

  • Pancake Batter
  • Milk or Water
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with the butter and spread it evenly
  2. While pan heats up mix flour with milk/water, slowly adding it to ensure consistency
  3. Add mixture to pan, ensuring there is a layer evenly spread across it
  4. Fry for about 2 mins each side or until golden
  5. Rinse and repeat for desired quality
