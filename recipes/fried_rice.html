Guide to an Excellent Fried Rice

Description

Worked on this easy to make recipe at school for nights in which I did not have much time. Or when I had a bit of extra overnight rice from other meals that I prepped before going to school

Ingredients

  • Overnight Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Protein of your Choice
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with the butter and spread it evenly
  2. Fry until gold the onions and garlic in the pan
  3. Throw in the protein of your choice and fry until cooked, separately Beat eggs
  4. In pan combine rice, eggs, protein, soy sauce
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste then serve
